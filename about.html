About


In modern society, countless people suffer from eating disorders. Although we tend to let go of the eating disorder as a light one, this can disastrously quietly ruin our daily lives or life in the long run. Also, not only can it have a significant physical impact, but it is also spiritually intimately linked. An appetite is one of humans' three basic needs: sleep and libido. Everyone has a different relationship with food. For something, it is a source of comfort, indulgence, or persistence. When these relationships are broken, food is no longer a pleasure but a sense of pain, guilt, and burden, just like endless tasks.


There are various types of eating disorders, but new categories are constantly being discovered and researched. Eating disorders are common health problems that occur in more than 5 percent of people worldwide, which can come at any moment in our lives. However, they are usually found in adolescence or young adulthood. The causes of eating disorders are a complex interaction of biological, psychological, behavioral, genetic, and social factors. However, this is one of the reasons for the tremendous pressure and stress of meeting the standards of a society that seeks perfection for women's beauty. In particular, women have higher rates of Anorexia nervosa, Bulemia nervosa, and Binge Eating Disorder than men.


This website showcases proven research materials and articles (healthline) focusing on women's eating disorders and the author's personal experience. It's just an archive of person who informs people of the severity of the eating disorder, raises awareness, and also supports "You" or "Someone" to overcome it. It's a space for the "You" of the community who are actually on their journey to recovery from an eating disorder and are trying to overcome it or wondering about it.
